Its strategic moves in blockchain integration could be a long-term positive, while short-term regulatory clarity is crucial.

However, analysts remain largely bullish, citing strong order books, supply shortages, impressive margins, and low valuations (for MU). The tug-of-war between these headwinds and fundamental strengths makes them volatile but potentially undervalued long-term plays if AI memory demand proves more resilient.
*   **Circle (CRCL):** After an initial dip based on what analysts deemed a "misread" of the CLARITY Act, its resilience and strong analyst backing make it an interesting watch for recovery and long-term growth in the stablecoin market. The next few weeks will show if the market corrects its initial reaction.
*   **Space Sector (Specifically public companies like Firefly Aerospace, Rocket Lab, Intuitive Machines, Planet Labs, Sidus Space, AST SpaceMobile):** The "SpaceX IPO halo effect" is significant. Continue to monitor these stocks as SpaceX's IPO process unfolds, as they could continue to benefit from increased investor interest in the space economy.
