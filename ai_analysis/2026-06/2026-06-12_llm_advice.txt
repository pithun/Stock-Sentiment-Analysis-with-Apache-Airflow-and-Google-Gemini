### 4. Stocks to Keep an Eye On

These stocks present interesting opportunities or critical developments that warrant close monitoring:

*   **SpaceX (SPCX):** While its IPO was a massive success, the long-term outlook is complex. Morningstar analysts value it at less than half its IPO price, citing high uncertainty and lack of profitability. Its reliance on "unproven technologies" and the "key-person dependency" on Elon Musk are significant risks. Watch how the stock stabilizes after the initial hype and if it can demonstrate a clearer path to profitability for its ambitious space and AI ventures.
*   **Oracle (ORCL):** The recent 22% sell-off, despite strong Q4 performance and raised FY27 guidance (to $90 billion revenue, 18% EPS growth), makes it a "classic contrarian entry." Its $638 billion contracted backlog and 93% cloud revenue growth suggest underlying strength that the market is currently overlooking. Monitor for a potential rebound as investors digest the strong fundamentals.
*   **Chewy (CHWY):** The company is facing a "belt-tightening reality" among pet owners, leading to lower sales guidance and a 54% stock drop. However, it's simultaneously becoming a "more efficient operation," expanding its adjusted EBITDA margins. Watch whether its efficiency gains can stabilize profitability and offset revenue pressures in a challenging consumer environment.
*   **Rocket Lab (RKLB) & AST SpaceMobile (ASTS):** These "space stocks" were negatively impacted by SpaceX's IPO, with RKLB down 8% and ASTS down 10%. However, Rocket Lab has strong fundamentals (record Q1 revenue, $2.2 billion backlog, NASDAQ 100 inclusion), and AST SpaceMobile has a critical satellite launch scheduled for next week, fueling speculative interest. Monitor if capital rotates back into these names once SpaceX's trading settles and how their upcoming catalysts play out.
*   **OpenAI & Anthropic:** These AI heavyweights are expected to have mega IPOs later this year. SpaceX's successful debut could "pave the way" for them. However, analysts caution about their "frothy valuation and hype" given they are currently "burning cash" and lack a "clear path to profitability." Watch for news on their IPO timing and how their profitability outlook evolves.
