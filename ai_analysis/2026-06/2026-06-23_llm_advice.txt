**Overall Investment Advice:**

The market is currently undergoing a "gut check moment" regarding the AI boom. While some analysts see this as a healthy, temporary pause to take profits after a historic run, others fear it might be the "beginning of the deflation of the AI bubble." Investors are increasingly demanding tangible returns and profits from AI investments, rather than just marketing buzz and projected growth.

Proceed with caution, prioritize companies with proven profitability or solid revenue backlogs (like Oracle), and closely monitor earnings reports (like Micron's tomorrow) for insights into the true state of AI demand and return on investment. The extreme volatility in newly public tech companies like SpaceX also underscores the speculative nature of parts of the current market.
