For long-term investors, keeping an eye on price dips could present a Buffett-approved entry point.
*   **Prediction Market Players (e.g., JPMorgan, Goldman Sachs, Coinbase, Robinhood):** The growing interest and entry of major financial institutions into prediction markets suggest this is a significant emerging sector. Watch how this space develops, especially regarding regulatory frameworks, as it could open new investment opportunities.
