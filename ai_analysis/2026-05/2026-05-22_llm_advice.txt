**4. Stocks to Keep an Eye On:**

1.  **SpaceX (SPCX):** Despite the "ghastly" financials and valuation concerns noted by analysts, SpaceX represents a monumental upcoming IPO with disruptive technologies (Starship, Starlink, xAI) and unprecedented ambitions. The sheer scale of its vision and its potential to revolutionize space and AI make it a critical watch, especially around its launch attempts and public market debut. Monitor how it addresses its significant losses and the market's reception of its high valuation.
2.  **Nvidia (NVDA):** As the undisputed leader in AI hardware, Nvidia is at the epicenter of a massive technological shift. While some market watchers draw parallels to the dot-com bubble, its consistent performance and essential role in AI infrastructure demand continued attention. Watch for sustained growth in data center revenue, the rollout of new chips, and any shifts in the competitive landscape.
3.  **BlackBerry (BB):** After years of struggle, BlackBerry is showing signs of a significant turnaround driven by its QNX automotive AI software. Its recent surge and strong performance in this segment make it an intriguing watch. Monitor its ability to sustain QNX's growth, secure more design wins, and convert its royalty backlog into profits, while being mindful of its relatively high forward P/E and historical underperformance.
4.  **Eli Lilly (LLY):** The pharmaceutical giant is experiencing a strong growth phase, primarily due to its GLP-1 medications for diabetes and weight loss. With new approvals like Foundayo, its drug pipeline is robust. Keep an eye on market adoption of these new treatments, manufacturing scaling, and potential competition in the rapidly expanding GLP-1 market.
