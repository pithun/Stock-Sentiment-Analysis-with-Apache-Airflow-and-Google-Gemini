**4. Stocks to Keep an Eye On:**

*   **Cipher Digital (CIFR):** This company is undergoing a massive transformation, pivoting from crypto mining to AI data centers with substantial long-term contracts. Its stock has seen incredible growth, but there are risks associated with high leverage and tenant concentration. The target of October 2026 for energization of its major sites is a critical upcoming catalyst.
*   **Paxos:** Its regulatory breakthrough as the first blockchain firm authorized as a central securities depository for traditional equities is a game-changer. This positions it to fundamentally alter post-trade frameworks and accelerate the institutional tokenization of real-world assets. A foundational shift could be underway.
*   **Strive Asset Management (ASST):** In contrast to Strategy (MSTR), Strive has shown strong performance, eliminated debt, and has an interesting, seemingly stabilizing, preferred stock structure with a high dividend yield. It's an intriguing alternative in the Bitcoin-linked investment space, demonstrating a different, potentially more robust, balance sheet strategy.
*   **Nvidia:** While Dell is skyrocketing partly due to Nvidia's AI chips, Nvidia's own stock has "barely inched upward" in recent months. This disconnect, noted as "irrationality" in the news, suggests either an undervaluation in Nvidia or an overvaluation in Dell (or a combination). It's crucial to monitor if Nvidia's stock starts to catch up with the underlying demand it's clearly generating.
*   **Samsung Electronics:** Beyond its AI chip endeavors, Samsung is leading the foldable smartphone market with significant growth in shipments and revenue. As this niche grows and potentially becomes mainstream, Samsung's dominance could provide sustained growth, making it a strong player to watch for diversified tech exposure.
