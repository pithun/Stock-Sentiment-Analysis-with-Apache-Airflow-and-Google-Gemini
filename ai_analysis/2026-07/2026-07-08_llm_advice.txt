### **4. Which Would You Advise Me to Keep an Eye On**

Given the current market dynamics, here are the stocks and themes I'd advise keeping a close eye on:

1.  **AI Infrastructure Plays (Marvell Technology, Nvidia, Micron, SK Hynix):**
    *   **Why:** Despite short-term volatility and some profit-taking (especially in memory), the fundamental demand for AI infrastructure remains exceptionally strong. These companies are at the core of this long-term trend.
    *   **Watch For:** Any dips could present buying opportunities, especially as institutional investors digest the SK Hynix IPO and reallocate capital. Marvell's continued acceleration in data center revenue and Nvidia's ecosystem expansion are key.

2.  **Eli Lilly (LLY):**
    *   **Why:** Its growth is "loud" and driven by blockbuster drugs like Mounjaro and Zepbound. Strong financial performance, increased guidance, and insider buying signal continued momentum.
    *   **Watch For:** Continued volume growth in its key products and any updates on a potential stock split, which could increase accessibility for retail investors.

3.  **Oracle (ORCL):**
    *   **Why:** Has quietly become a major AI infrastructure player with a massive revenue backlog and impressive IaaS growth. Currently trades at a discounted forward P/E.
    *   **Watch For:** Improvement in its cash conversion and management of CapEx. If free cash flow issues resolve and the AI thesis fully plays out, its current valuation could represent a significant upside.

4.  **T-Mobile US (TMUS):**
    *   **Why:** In a volatile market, its stability, consistent dividend, strong free cash flow, and low beta make it an attractive option, especially for income-focused or more conservative investors.
    *   **Watch For:** Continued dividend growth and share buybacks, and its ability to maintain subscriber growth amidst competition.

5.  **Crude Oil Prices and Related Stocks:**
    *   **Why:** Geopolitical tensions have made crude oil a primary market driver. Its trajectory will significantly impact inflation, consumer discretionary spending, and sectors like airlines.
    *   **Watch For:** Escalations or de-escalations in the Middle East, and their direct impact on energy companies and indirect effects on the broader economy.

6.  **Meta & Tesla (Post-Earnings):**
    *   **Why:** Both are large-cap tech names facing specific challenges (Meta: AI spending ROI; Tesla: stagnant sales/competition) but also have significant potential if they execute well.
    *   **Watch For:** Their upcoming earnings reports in July will be critical tests of their strategies and profitability. Meta's AI product revenue and Tesla's vehicle delivery numbers will be key.

7.  **Tokenized Stocks (Securitize, Figure, xStocks, Ondo):**
    *   **Why:** This nascent market is showing exponential growth and increasing institutional interest. It represents a potential paradigm shift in capital markets.
    *   **Watch For:** Further regulatory clarity, more mainstream adoption, and continued innovation from platforms and exchanges. This is a longer-term, higher-risk, higher-reward area.
