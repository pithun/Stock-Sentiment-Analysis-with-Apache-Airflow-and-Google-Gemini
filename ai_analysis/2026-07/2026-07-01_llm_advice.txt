**Investment Advice:**

Today's news highlights a stark contrast in the market, particularly within the tech and semiconductor sectors. While some companies like **Meta** and **Axsome Therapeutics** are showing strong, fundamentally-backed growth, there's significant cautionary sentiment surrounding the broader **AI stock bubble**. Michael Burry's aggressive short positions against industry giants like **Tesla**, **Nvidia**, and key semiconductor ETFs (SOXX) should not be ignored, echoing Bank of America's warnings.

*   **Proceed with caution** in highly-valued AI-related stocks, as the "bubble risk" narrative is gaining traction. The significant drops in companies like TSM, AMD, and Intel after BofA's comments, and Broadcom despite strong earnings, demonstrate this sensitivity.
*   **Look for companies with clear, demonstrated growth catalysts** beyond just "AI hype," like **Axsome Therapeutics** with its drug approvals and pipeline, or **Meta** leveraging AI into new revenue streams.
*   **Be aware of company-specific risks** like the merger termination for **Getty Images** and **Shutterstock**, which led to substantial share price declines.
*   **MicroStrategy** and **Broadcom** present interesting "watch-and-wait" scenarios. Broadcom's strong underlying business is attractive, but its valuation is clearly under intense scrutiny. MicroStrategy's story is more about external factors and potential turnaround.

As always, conduct your own thorough research and consider your personal risk tolerance before making any investment decisions.
