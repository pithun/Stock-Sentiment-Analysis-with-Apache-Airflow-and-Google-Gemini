#### **4. Stocks to Keep an Eye On**

*   **SpaceX:** While its IPO was record-breaking and initial shares soared, it faces significant risks. JPMorgan warned of high volatility, massive cash burn, and operating losses. Critics are concerned about its unusual, complex lock-up schedules which could lead to significant share price pressure if insiders sell. Its "vibes-based accounting" also raises questions about valuation vs. actual financial results. **Watch for:** how its stock navigates these lock-up periods and whether its cash burn and profitability improve as xAI develops.
*   **Bitcoin (BTC) & the broader Crypto Market (Ether, ADA, Zcash, Dash, Solana):** The crypto market is recovering, with Bitcoin hitting new July highs and altcoins participating in the rally, fueled by weak US job data easing rate hike fears. However, the broader market structure for most tokens remains bearish, with weak institutional demand and ETF outflows. The RBI's stance that cryptocurrency is a "threat" to India adds regulatory uncertainty. Solana shows strong internal metrics (tokenized assets, active addresses) but faces skepticism about a sustained rally based solely on temporary memecoin demand. **Watch for:** sustained momentum above key resistance levels ($62K, $65K for BTC), clearer signs of institutional adoption, and regulatory developments, especially the RBI's influence in India.
*   **OpenAI:** Its IPO plans seem shaken by SpaceX's volatile debut. Advisors are cautioning against moving too soon due to market skepticism of high valuations, lack of "Musk-driven attention," and operating at a massive financial loss. **Watch for:** any new IPO timelines, signs of improved financial performance, and how it differentiates itself in a market wary of highly-valued, unprofitable AI companies.
*   **Preferred Stock ETFs (PFF, PFXF, PFFA):** These ETFs offer benefits like diversification and monthly income. PFF has a high concentration in financial institutions, which might be a concern for some investors. PFFA uses leverage to boost income but magnifies losses and has higher fees. **Watch for:** how these funds perform under varying interest rate environments, especially PFFA with its leverage, and how PFXF continues to offer diversified exposure outside financials.
*   **OneSource Specialty Pharma:** While shares were flat, a USFDA cGMP inspection of its Bengaluru facility resulted in "one observation." While not immediately severe, such observations can sometimes lead to future compliance issues or delays if not addressed. **Watch for:** follow-up information regarding this observation and its potential impact on operations or product approvals.
